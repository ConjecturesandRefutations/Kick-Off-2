The object of the game is to direct the ball into the farthest goal whilst avoiding the opponents.

For each goal you score, your score increases by 1.

Each time you hit into an opponent (the players on screen), their score increases by one.

Try to score as many goals whilst limiting the opponents' goals.

Desktop/Laptop Controls

Use your keyboard's arrow keys to move the ball.

Mobile/Tablet Controls

Use the on-screen arrows to move the ball.
